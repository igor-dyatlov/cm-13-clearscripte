# Nexusfication
Cleaning script for custom firmware. Replacing files ringtones, butanimatsii, configuration files on the stock of official images.

## Support devices
- Galaxy Nexus
- Nexus 4
- Nexus 5
- Nexus 6
- Nexus 5x
- Nexus 6p
- Nexus 7
- Nexus 9
